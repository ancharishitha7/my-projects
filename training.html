Step1
Decide what you want to achieve: weight loss, muscle gain, endurance, flexibility, or overall health. Make goals SMART: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound.
Step2
Check your current fitness level (e.g., endurance, strength, flexibility, BMI). Consider consulting a fitness professional or doing self-assessments like push-up tests or running time trials.
Step3
Stay Consistent Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week (per WHO guidelines).
Step4
Sleep 7–9 hours per night. Allow muscle recovery time (especially after strength training).
Step5
Keep a fitness journal or use a fitness app. Monitor improvements in stamina, strength, and flexibility.
Step6
Don’t ignore pain—rest or seek medical advice if needed. Adapt your routine to your energy levels, schedule, and health needs.
